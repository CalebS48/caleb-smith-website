About Me

Hello! I'm Caleb. My journey into engineering started with a childhood fascination for how things work, which has evolved into a dedicated pursuit of building elegant and efficient mechanical systems, with specific interests in Audio-Visual systems and the themed entertainment industry.

At UCF, I serve as the Senior Technician for the Student Union’s Audio Visual department. We manage all AV systems within the facility, ensuring both student-led and professional events function seamlessly. In this role, I have taken on the highest student leadership position, acting as the primary technical authority and mentor for the entire staff. My responsibilities extend beyond high-level troubleshooting to include team operations and workforce management, where I develop staff schedules to ensure comprehensive technical coverage for every event. In addition to my technical role, I serve on the committee board for UCF's ASME chapter, where I manage social media presence and design promotional materials for our engineering events. Managing these complex systems and large teams has taught me that technical expertise is most effective when paired with clear communication and a proactive approach to problem-solving.
